LIME@USC


Language, intelligence, and model evaluation (LIME ) lab is part of the USC NLP Group.

Hello! Welcome to LIME lab! Our team's primary focus is on creating trustworthy models. We investigate how trustworthy or reliable large models could be, striving to ensure that language technologies are constructed and employed in ways that align with human values. Please read our Group Wiki if you are interested working with us.

I'm actively looking for strong and motivated students. We welcome applicants from diverse backgrounds. If you are interested in my research, please fill in this form.
